Our Services

Patent drawings are an essential part of any successful patent application. The patent drawing details the unique features of your invention, and it is your patent drawing which enters the official record at the USPTO. Patent drawings are required for both design and utility patents. Through our patenting service, we connect you with expert illustrators with experience in patent drawings. Our experienced Draftsman are capable of complex hand and computer-aided drawings. We can work from most any type of inputs such as photos, hand sketches, any 3d format, Inventors disclosures, blue prints, prototypes or computer-aided drawings in most any format.
