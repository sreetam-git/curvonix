About us

CURVONIX goal is to offer quality patent drawings service, in a timely manner, and at a reasonable cost to the inventers. CURVONIX is a full service patent Illustration company located in INDIA that serves companies and patent law firms across the US. You will learn that our draftsman have outstanding illustration skills and expertise in the latest illustration technology. We are qualified to deliver anything from a simple flow chart to even the most complex mechanical cases and design case applications.

CURVONIX has been in the quality service provider role since 2016. Currently operating 24/7 at 365 days, CURVONIX is looking to establish its client base all over the world. Being a provider for multiple services CURVONIX has a staff of professional artists. Having experience prior to their time at CURVONIX, artists at CURVONIX are familiar with using their skills at full efficiency and constructing a final product according to the client’s instructions.

Specializing in their roles an illustrator and 3D model provider, CURVONIX has a team of talented and professional artists who have been in the patent provider market for – years. Supported by our team of artists, CURVONIX guarantees quality driven excellent work to its clients. Providing services for their clients, CURVONIX covers its client's needs for flow charts, screenshots, graphs, utility drawings, mechanical drawings, electric circuit drawings, engineering drawings, design patents, trademark, chemical and 3D models. Along with their services CURVONIX focuses on client satisfaction and maximum service adequacy. Gearing towards providing more and more clients with their services, CURVONIX is always welcoming to new clients and is open to accepting every type of service order
